Week 10

Design lecture

During this lecture Rich talked to us about design quality, intent, patterns, MVC Design Pattern, Information Design Model, Interaction Design View, Interraction Design Controller, Technical&Architectural Design.

It’s been discussed what understanding the user’s requirements means and how to deliver what is requested. Understanding what is good enough given certain constraints that limit what we can achieve being willing to accept a “good enough” solution, even if it may not be our preference.

We talked about the constraints that technology sometimes gives us, being a master in a technology sometimes means being a master of being constrained, being pushed to think in a certain way or all the ways a certain system allows us to think.

The importance of making right choices using the right information has been discussed along how to design interfaces so that it can be difficult for a user to make a mistake
