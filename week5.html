Week 5

Academic writing

In the Academic writing lecture we’ve been given tips about what sort of language to avoid in our literature reviews and on what to concentrate, we’ve also been given many examples about different types of writing, such as journal article, letters etc..

During the practical we did some basic exercises like Replacing Phrasal Verbs with Cues, Replacing Phrasal Verbs without Cues, Choosing Appropriate Adjectives/Expressions, Choosing Formal Words and Phrases, Contraction Alternatives, Avoiding Personal and Subjective Writing, Exercise Caution, Accurate Formal Writing.
